Privacy Policy

At QuickBook, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information while providing our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, etc.
  • Appointment Details: Date, time, and type of appointment.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, and usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide and manage appointment services.
  • Send reminders and updates.
  • Improve our platform and user experience.

3. Data Security

We implement advanced security measures to protect your personal data. This includes encryption, secure servers, and restricted access.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your information with unauthorized third parties. Data may only be shared with service providers necessary to fulfill your appointments.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@quickbook.com to make requests.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
